Project: VEX Robotics Competition-StarStruck
Duration: one year
Objective: Build a robot that throws foam stars and cubes over a fence as fast as possible
Type of Project: Team Project

In this project I was mainly a builder. My Job was to Build and upkeep our robot over time as well as be able to come up with solutions to problems before they even happen. This Project is a Competition between many different teams arround the world, all whom follow THIS rule book which describes how points will be counted as well as defines what you can and cannot do to create your robot. We have documented our entire design progress throught the year HERE. You can see the Reveal for our Final Robot for this season HERE. In this project many people from different backrounds came together to form a well rounded team. We need many different types of people such as Programmers, Builders, Computer aided designers, Documentors, and many more sub catagories exist.
